### Manual Installation (Developer Mode / Open Source)
1. Download the latest `release_v1.0.0.zip` from the button above.
2. Extract the ZIP file into a folder on your computer.
3. Open Google Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions/`.
4. Enable **"Developer mode"** in the top right corner.
5. Click **"Load unpacked"** and select the folder you just extracted.
6. The extension is now active on your Chrome!

## ✨ Key Features
- 🚀 **One-Click Download**: Instantly adds a "Quick Download" button to Gemini-generated images. Download pristine, unwatermarked originals in seconds.
- 🛠️ **Built-in Power Editor**: Not just a downloader! Click "Edit" to crop, rotate, flip, and adjust aspect ratios directly within your browser.
- 💾 **Multiple Export Formats**: Save your final tailored image as PNG, JPEG, or WebP.
- 🔒 **Privacy First**: Everything is processed locally on your hardware. No data or images are ever uploaded to any external servers.

## 💡 How to Use
1. Navigate to [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) and ask the AI to generate an image.
2. Hover your mouse over the generated image to reveal two newly injected buttons: **Quick Download** and **Edit**.
3. Click **Quick Download** to extract the unwatermarked original immediately.
4. *Need the absolute maximum resolution?* Use Gemini's native download button, then click on this Extension's icon in your browser toolbar to quickly process that high-quality file in one click!
