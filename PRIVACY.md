# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 16, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use **Image Downloader for Gemini - No Watermark** ("the Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure experience.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
We strongly believe in data privacy. The Extension **does not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user profiling data with any external servers or third parties. 

## 2. How the Extension Works (Local Processing & AI Upscaling)
The core functionalities of the Extension, including fetching images, removing watermarks, and applying basic image edits (cropping, rotating), are performed **entirely locally** on your device within your web browser. 
- Temporary image data (such as blobs or base64 structures) used during the editing process is stored strictly within the browser's temporary memory and is automatically cleared shortly after use.

**AI Image Upscaling feature:**
If you choose to use the optional "AI Upscale" feature introduced in version 1.1.0, the specific image you are editing will be temporarily uploaded to a third-party API service (`imglarger.com` / `imgupscaler.com`) solely for the purpose of enlarging and enhancing the image. 
- These images are processed securely and are generally deleted by the service provider shortly after processing. 
- If you do not click the "Upscale" button, your images will **never** leave your local browser.

## 3. Permissions Justification
To function correctly, the Extension requires the following minimalistic permissions:
- **`storage`**: Used exclusively to save your local extension preferences (e.g., keeping the Extension toggle ON or OFF) and temporarily cache image files to pass them from the Gemini page to the editing interface seamlessly.
- **`host_permissions`** (`*://*.googleusercontent.com/*`, `*://gemini.google.com/*`, `*://get1.imglarger.com/*`, etc.): Required strictly to fetch the image files generated by you on Google's servers, and to communicate with the third-party AI upscaler API if triggered by the user.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Extension interacts directly with Google's Gemini interface to provide its functionality. For the AI Upscaling feature, it interacts with third-party image enhancement API endpoints. It does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or ad networks. 

## 5. Changes to This Policy
If we introduce new features that require a change in our data practices, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and ensure full transparency.
