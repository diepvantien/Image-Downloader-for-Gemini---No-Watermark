# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use **Image Downloader for Gemini - No Watermark** ("the Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure experience.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
We strongly believe in data privacy. The Extension **does not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user data with any external servers or third parties. 

## 2. How the Extension Works (Local Processing)
All core functionalities of the Extension, including fetching images, removing watermarks, and applying image edits, are performed **entirely locally** on your device within your web browser. 
- The images you download or edit are never uploaded to any remote server by our Extension.
- Temporary image data (such as blobs or base64 structures) used during the editing process is stored strictly within the browser's temporary memory and is automatically cleared shortly after use.

## 3. Permissions Justification
To function correctly, the Extension requires the following minimalistic permissions:
- **`storage`**: Used exclusively to save your local extension preferences (e.g., keeping the Extension toggle ON or OFF) and temporarily cache image files to pass them from the Gemini page to the editing interface seamlessly.
- **`host_permissions`** (`*://*.googleusercontent.com/*`, `*://gemini.google.com/*`, etc.): Required strictly to fetch the image files generated by you on Google's servers so they can be downloaded or edited directly by the Extension.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Extension interacts directly with Google's Gemini interface to provide its functionality. It does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or ad networks. 

## 5. Changes to This Policy
If we introduce new features that require a change in our data practices, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and ensure full transparency.
